The Xiaomi Redmi Note 11 Pro flaunts a 6.67-inch IPS LCD display with Full HD+ resolution. The device uses an AMOLED panel that has a 20:9 aspect ratio and a pixel density of 396 PPI. Powering the handset under the hood, there's an octa-core Dimensity 920 chipset. The device ships with 6GB of RAM and packs 128GB of internal storage. As for the software, the Redmi Note 11 Pro runs Android 11 out of the box with MIUI 12.5 skin on top. In the optics department, the device features a triple-camera setup that consists of a 108MP primary camera, 8MP ultra-wide lens, and a 2MP depth sensor. For the selfie purpose, the handset sports a 16MP camera. The device features a side-mounted fingerprint sensor for security purpose. Connectivity-wise the device has dual-SIM support, 5G, 4G VoLTE, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth v5.1, GPS, a 3.5mm audio jack, and a USB Type-C port. The other popular mobiles from Xiaomi include the Redmi Note 10 and the Redmi Note 10 Pro Max.
